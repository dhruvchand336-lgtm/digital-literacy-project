Stay Safe Online – Prevention Tips

- Never share your UPI PIN with anyone
- Do not scan unknown QR codes
- Always verify the sender before making payments
- Do not click on suspicious links
- Use strong passwords for all accounts
- Enable two-factor authentication
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for transactions
- Regularly check your bank statements

UPI Safety:
- Remember that receiving money does not require entering a PIN
- Reject unknown payment requests immediately

Report Cybercrime:
- Website: cybercrime.gov.in
- Helpline: 1930
